Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, select the items you'd like to order, and proceed to checkout. You can choose delivery or pickup options based on your preference.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and cash on delivery. You can choose the most convenient option for you during checkout.

What should I do if I receive the wrong order or items are missing?

If you receive the wrong order or items are missing from your order, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will investigate the issue and take appropriate action to resolve it as quickly as possible.

How can I provide feedback or suggestions?

We welcome feedback and suggestions from our customers. You can submit your feedback through our website's contact form, email, or social media channels. Your input helps us improve our services and offerings.
